About Me

Energetic and hard-working undergraduate concentrating in American Studies and Journalism. Passionate follower of college and professional sports, with experience broadcasting for Fighting Irish Media and athletic coverage for The Notre Dame Observer and MyRye.com. Multi-year hockey blogger with the ability to deliver thoughtful content that engages reader enthusiasm and builds community.

I have over 200 hours of experience behind the microphone as a sports broadcaster across six different varsity sports (volleyball, rugby, hockey, women's basketball, baseball, and softball). I have experience in both play-by-play and color commentary roles, both on and off camera, and for both radio and television audiences. I have commentated full-day rugby events multiple times, from 4 full-length matches to 16 7-on-7 games.
