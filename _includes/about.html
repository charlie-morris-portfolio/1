About Me

Energetic and hard-working undergraduate concentrating in American Studies and Journalism at the University of Notre Dame. Passionate follower of college and professional sports, with experience broadcasting for Fighting Irish Media and athletic coverage for The Notre Dame Observer and MyRye.com. Multi-year hockey blogger with the ability to deliver thoughtful content that engages reader enthusiasm and builds community.

I have over 250 hours of experience behind the microphone as a sports broadcaster across nine different varsity sports (volleyball, men's rugby, women's rugby, hockey, women's basketball, men's soccer, baseball, and softball). I have experience in play-by-play, color commentary, on-camera opens, radio broadcasts, and television shows. I have commentated full-day rugby events multiple times, including 4 full-length matches, 16 7-on-7 games, and the 2025 NCR championships.
